How It Works

Starting gear

Each player starts with 1 boat, 5 pots, and $0.

Boat capacity

Each boat can hold up to 10 pots in a round.

Good weather

Inside reef earns $2 per pot. Outside reef earns $8 per pot.

Bad weather

Inside reef earns $4 per pot. Outside reef pots are lost.

Buying

Extra boats cost $100. Extra pots cost $5.

Selling

Boats sell back for $50. Pots sell back for $5.
